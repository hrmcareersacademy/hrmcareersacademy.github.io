About HRM Careers Academy

HRM Careers Academy was founded by Nqobile Charmaine Dube with the vision to empower individuals across South Africa and beyond to earn income by teaching English online and building successful remote careers.

We are a 100% Black Female-Owned, Level 1 B-BBEE training institution providing virtual learning opportunities designed to help individuals develop essential teaching and digital communication skills. Our academy believes that every person can thrive in the online workspace with the right tools, guidance, and confidence.

Through our accredited programs and mentorship, we help aspiring online educators become professional, independent, and globally competitive.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through accessible, affordable, and high-quality online training that opens doors to global remote career opportunities.

Our Vision

To be South Africa’s leading academy for online teaching and remote work readiness by 2030.
