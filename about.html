About HRM Careers Academy

HRM Careers Academy was founded by Nqobile Charmaine Dube, an experienced online educator and certified TEFL instructor with a passion for helping others discover the freedom and possibilities of working remotely.

After years of teaching students locally and through one-on-one virtual lessons, Nqobile saw the growing need for people to learn how to teach English online, develop digital skills, and earn a sustainable income from home.

HRM Careers Academy was created to make that vision a reality — to open doors for individuals who dream of independence, flexibility, and professional growth through online education.

We provide practical, accessible, and motivational training designed to build confidence, skills, and opportunity in the digital workspace.

Mission

To empower and inspire individuals through online education, helping them create sustainable income and a brighter future.

Vision

To be a leading virtual academy that transforms lives through accessible learning and global career opportunities.

Values

Empowerment • Growth • Excellence • Integrity
